TuneAndTime Privacy Policy

Effective Date:25 Novemver, 2025

Information We Collect

  • No personal data is collected — you don’t need an account to use TuneAndTime.
  • Microphone access is used only for tuning. Audio is processed locally and never stored or shared.
  • Preferences (like metronome settings) are saved only on your device.
  • Optional anonymous analytics may be used to improve performance, but never linked to you.

How We Use Information

We use information solely to provide accurate tuner and metronome functionality, improve app performance, and troubleshoot technical issues. We do not sell or share your information.

Data Storage & Security

All processing happens locally on your device. If analytics are enabled, data is anonymized and aggregated. We implement reasonable safeguards to protect your information.

Children’s Privacy

TuneAndTime is safe for all ages. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Updates will be posted within the app or on our official site.
